Ayurvedic Body Types (Tridosha)

We are all made of 5 elements. Their unique combination defines you.

Vata (Air + Ether)

Creative, energetic, but prone to anxiety. Like the wind, they are always moving. Needs warmth and routine.

Pitta (Fire + Water)

Intelligent, sharp, but prone to anger. Like fire, they transform things. Needs cooling foods and calmness.

Kapha (Earth + Water)

Calm, loyal, strong, but prone to lethargy. Like the earth, they are stable. Needs stimulation and exercise.
