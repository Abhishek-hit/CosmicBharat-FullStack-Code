Engineering Marvels

Konark Sun Temple

Odisha

Built in the 13th century, this temple is designed as a giant chariot. Its 24 wheels are actually sundials that can calculate time accurately to the minute, day, and night.

Brihadeeswarar Temple

Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu

The world's first complete granite temple. The Kumbam (apex stone) weighs 80 tons. How was it lifted 216 feet high in 1010 AD without cranes? Likely using a 6km long ramp.

Kailasa Temple

Ellora, Maharashtra

The only temple in the world carved from the top down out of a single rock mountain. 200,000 tons of rock were removed with extreme precision. A mystery of civil engineering.
