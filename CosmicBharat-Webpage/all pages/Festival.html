Science of Celebration

January 14/15

Makar Sankranti

Winter Solstice Transition

Hover to Reveal Logic
Scientific Logic:

Marks the sun's entry into Capricorn (Makara). Days become longer. Eating Sesame (Til) and Jaggery provides essential heat and oil to survive the last phase of winter.

Oct / Nov

Deepawali

New Moon & Fumigation

Hover to Reveal Logic
Scientific Logic:

Falls after the rainy season when insect breeding is high. Lighting oil lamps and cleaning homes acts as a massive communal fumigation drive to kill bacteria.

Seasonal Shifts

Navratri

Detoxification (Fasting)

Hover to Reveal Logic
Scientific Logic:

Occurs during major seasonal changes when immunity is low. Fasting (Sattvic diet) gives the digestive system a break, cleanses toxins, and boosts immunity.
