Nadi Stuti The Sacred Rivers

Sindhu

Modern: Indus River

The cradle of the Indus Valley Civilization. The name 'India' and 'Hindu' are geographically derived from this river.

View on Map

Saraswati

Modern: Lost / Ghaggar

The mightiest river of the Rigveda. It dried up around 1900 BCE due to tectonic shifts, leading to the decline of Harappan cities.

Read History

Rewa

Modern: Narmada

One of the oldest rivers in India. Fossils found in the Narmada valley suggest human habitation for over 100,000 years.
